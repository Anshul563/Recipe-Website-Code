Matar Paneer

Here's a simple and delicious Matar Paneer recipe for you, step-by-step:

Ingredients

  • Paneer - 200g (Cubed)
  • Green peas (matar) - 1 cup (fresh or frozen)
  • Onions - 2 large, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 large, pureed
  • Green chili - 1 (optional, slit)
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1 tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Fresh cream - 2 tbsp (optional for creaminess)
  • Oil or ghee - 2-3 tbsp
  • Coriander leaves - for garnish

Preparation:

  • Cut paneer into cubes and set aside.
  • Finely chop the onions, puree the tomatoes, and keep them ready.

Saute Paneer:

  • Heat 1 tbsp oil or ghee in a pan.
  • Add the paneer cubes and lightly saute them until golden on each side. Remove from the pan and set aside.

Cook the Masala:

  • In the same pan, add a bit more oil or ghee if needed.
  • Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and saute until they turn golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili. Saute until the raw smell disappears.

Add Tomato Puree & Spices:

  • Pour in the tomato puree and cook until it thickens and releases oil.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few more minutes.

Cook the Peas:

  • Add the green peas (if using fresh, you may need to cook a bit longer until they soften).
  • Add 1/2 cup of water, cover the pan, and let it simmer for 5 minutes.

Add Paneer & Garam Masala:

  • Add the paneer cubes to the pan and gently stir.
  • Sprinkle garam masala and mix.
  • Simmer for 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to combine.

Add Cream (Optional):

  • For a creamier texture, stir in fresh cream and mix well.

Garnish and Serve:

  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
