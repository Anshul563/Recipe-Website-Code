Welcome to VeggeiBites!

I’m Anshul Shakya, a BCA student at GLA University, and the creator of VegeeiBites. This website is my way of sharing a passion for vegetarian cooking with others, showing that healthy, plant-based meals can be easy, flavorful, and exciting.


On VegeeiBites, you’ll find a variety of recipes designed to make vegetarian cooking accessible and enjoyable for everyone, from beginners to seasoned cooks. Each recipe is crafted with fresh ingredients and inspired by a love for global flavors and healthy living.



Thank you for visiting VegeeiBites, and I hope you find recipes here that you’ll love to make and share!
